20 Greek Recipes That Are Perfect for Summer Dinners

These recipes are simple, top-rated, and vibrant.

By
Carl Hanson
Carl Hanson

Carl Hanson is a Senior Editor at Allrecipes who has been writing about food and wine for nearly 20 years. He enjoys creating content that informs, entertains, and assists busy home cooks get nourishing meals on the table for their families.

Allrecipes' editorial guidelines
Published on July 25, 2024

Summer is the right season for exploring Greek cuisine. After all, Greek cooking makes the most of fresh produce and clean bright flavors. It's also a Mediterranean cuisine that really rewards simplicity. Give these top-rated recipes for vibrant Greek salads and simple grilled chicken, fish, and lamb dishes a try.

01 of 20

Greek Horiatiki Salad

This Greek summer salad is best with ripe, red tomatoes. "This is the one and only Greek salad recipe that you'll ever need!" says Editorial Director Diana Moutsopoulos. "Enjoy this Greek salad for summer BBQs, Greek dinner parties, picnics, and more will yield the best result. Serve with loads of good crusty bread to mop up the gorgeous juices."

View Recipe
02 of 20

Mediterranean Orzo Salad

"This light and refreshing cold pasta salad is loaded with the flavors of the Mediterranean, and pairs well with grilled proteins, such as fish, pork, chicken or steak," says Kim's Cooking Now. "If you make this in advance, reserve a bit of the dressing to add right before serving, as the pasta has a tendency to soak up the dressing as it sits over time."

View Recipe
03 of 20

Greek Island Chicken Shish Kebabs

Give your chicken a quick dip in a Greek-inspired mixture of olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, and herbs before threading them onto skewers with bell pepper, mushrooms, and tomatoes. "This is a nice change from the ordinary grilled chicken," according to recipe creator Dolce Cuoco. "This is excellent served with pita bread, a garlicky tzatziki, and a Greek salad."

View Recipe
04 of 20

Branzino Mediterranean

"This simple, healthy, and delicious Mediterranean fish, also known as 'Greek Sea Bass,' should be cooked whole," says Christala. "The cavity of the fish can be stuffed with lemon and other ingredients for added flavor. It is a very mild-tasting and versatile fish. The oregano and lemon make it truly Greek cuisine that many will enjoy. Can be found wild-caught in many stores. Enjoy!"

05 of 20

My Big Fat Greek Salad

This Greek salad combines fresh summer tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, bell peppers, Kalamata olives, oregano, and feta cheese along with a simple bright vinaigrette. "You can't go wrong with this Greek salad," says Chef John, "especially if you remember the only and most important tip: toss it with the vinegar first before adding olive oil."

View Recipe
06 of 20

Skordalia (Greek Garlic Potato Dip)

To make this famous Greek dip, just smash boiled potatoes and garlic into a paste, along with lemon juice, vinegar, and olive oil. And then, to develop the unique luxurious texture, you'll press them through a mesh strainer. "Not only is this a fabulous dip for vegetables and breads and things like that, it's also served as a side dish for things like fried fish and grilled meatsâ€”so this stuff is as versatile as it is easy to make," says Chef John.

View Recipe
07 of 20

Grilled Greek Chicken

The secret to this simple Greek-inspired grilled chicken is "a very powerful marinade and 'roasting' it slowly over semi-indirect heat on the grill," says Chef John.

View Recipe
08 of 20

Lamb Souvlaki

"Tender pieces of lamb are marinated in a Greek lemon vinaigrette, threaded on skewers, and char-grilled to perfection," says Kim's Cooking Now. "I like to serve these with rosemary garlic roasted potatoes, a Greek salad, and pita bread."

View Recipe
09 of 20

Greek Chicken and Potato Bowl

This Greek-inspired meal is like a mashup of two classic Chef John recipes: his classic Greek chicken and potatoes recipe combined with his Greek salad recipe. It's a complete meal in a bowl. "My family LOVED this!" raves CathyandRob Smith. "It reminds us of the time we were in Greece, eating at the delicious Greek restaurants!"

View Recipe
10 of 20

Greek Farro Salad

"This is a great alternative to a cold pasta salad as farro doesn't get soggy and holds up to the dressing very well," says WestCoastMom. "Farro is an ancient Mediterranean grain, so I wanted to merge it with a Greek-style salad that's perfect for summertime. This is great served with grilled lamb or chicken. Add black olives or fresh herbs to your liking!"

View Recipe
11 of 20

Greek Style Garlic Chicken Breast

This simple Greek-inspired chicken recipe features a simple marinade made of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and paprika. "This recipe was passed down through my family for years," says Michael. "If you're looking for a great tasting, tender chicken breast, this is what you're looking forâ€”Greek style."

View Recipe
12 of 20

Chef John's Lamb Moussaka Burger

Not traditional, of course, but you don't want to miss Chef John's amazing moussaka makeover. "I've been daydreaming recently about a lamb burger based on the famous Greek casserole Moussaka," says Chef John. "For those of you who don't know, moussaka is a stunning blend of spiced ground lamb, tomato, and eggplant, topped with a creamy, cheesy white sauce. I decided to integrate the eggplant right into the burger itself, instead of topping the burger with cooked slices."

Orzo and Tomato Salad with Feta Cheese

"A cold orzo feta tomato salad with orzo, green olives, feta cheese, parsley, dill, tomato, olive oil, and lemon juice. If you don't have fresh herbs, use 1 teaspoon dried. As a personal chef, I can let you know that my clients love this dish," says COZYCUISINE.

14 of 20

Chicken Souvlaki with Tzatziki Sauce

These seasoned Greek kabobs are served with a simple homemade tzatziki sauce. "Love this recipe!" raves BusyMom. "It came out wonderful! Served with pita, tomatoes, and red onions. Delicious!"

15 of 20

Greek Stuffed Tomatoes and Peppers (Yemista)

Have some extra summer tomatoes and peppers on hand? "The best time to make it is in August when the garden tomatoes are ready for harvest," says Denise Phillips Sarigianopoulo. "My mom made this every year, and when she did, I thought I died and went to heaven."

View Recipe
16 of 20

Maroulosalata (Greek Romaine Salad)

"The simple ingredient list shouldn't fool you â€” it's such a light, crunchy, refreshing salad that is full of flavor," says Editorial Director Diana Moutsopoulos. "In Greece, maroulosalata is one of the most common salads. I particularly love it alongside roast or grilled meats. The key to authenticity is to almost shred the romaine lettuce (marouli), rather than tear it into large pieces as we normally do in the States."

Kolokithokeftedes (Greek Zucchini Fritters)

When the end of summer gives you mountains of fresh zucchini, you'll need this recipe. Pan fry these bread crumb-coated fritters in olive oil.

18 of 20

Frozen Greek Yogurt

Take the edge off the summer heat with this frozen Greek yogurt flavored with minted honey. "Cold, smooth, and refreshing," says Beckerkorn. "Terrific for summer evenings. Garnish with mint sprigs, orange wheels, or strawberry slices."

19 of 20

Briam (Greek Baked Zucchini and Potatoes)

"This briam recipe makes a roasted vegetable dish with potatoes, zucchini, tomatoes, red onions, and lots of olive oil," says Editorial Director Diana Moutsopoulos. "It is a typical example of Greek cuisine where a few simple ingredients are turned into an utterly delicious dish with little effort. Briam can be served as a main course with a hearty chunk of feta on the side."
